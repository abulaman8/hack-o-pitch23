Runner Up

Our team secured second place at the event. While we may not have achieved first place, we are still proud of the effort, work, dedication, and commitment we put in to get this far. We designed the app at Hack-O-Pitch to simplify attendance-taking in classrooms using ultrasonic sounds. With this app, teachers can take attendance with the push of a button on their mobile device. This app can save time and eliminate manual attendance-taking, making it an excellent tool for busy teachers and professors. This event has been a valuable experience for us all, and we've learned many meaningful lessons along the way. We will use this as motivation to continue to improve and strive for excellence in everything we do.


2nd Runner Up

I am thankful to be the representative of our team who placed third in this event. Despite not taking the top two spots, we are still proud of our performance and appreciative of the chance to compete. "Orion" Backend as a Service using Decentralised Storage [IPFS]. We built a decentralized PAAS using IPFS. This is a secure distributed file system using the Interplanetary File System protocol. We have addressed this issue because PAAS gets expensive when scaling the application, and centralized services introduce a single point of failure. With Orion, users can enjoy secure, distributed file storage without vendor locking. Our platform offers a range of features, including buckets, log store, key value store, authentication, document storage, and analytics. With Orion, you have complete control over your data and can scale your applications without breaking the bank.
